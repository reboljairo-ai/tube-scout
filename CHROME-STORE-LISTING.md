## DESCRIPCIÓN COMPLETA (pegar tal cual en el formulario)

Find your next winning YouTube niche with real data.

TubeScout is a research tool for YouTube creators, marketers, and agencies. It surfaces the data you need to identify content opportunities, analyze what's working in any niche, and make informed decisions before investing time in a new channel or video topic.

---

**WHAT TUBESCOUT DOES**

🔍 Niche Analyzer
Enter any topic or keyword to get an opportunity score (0–100), estimated monthly revenue range, engagement benchmarks, and the top-performing videos in that space. Understand at a glance whether a niche is worth entering or already saturated.

📈 Viral Content Tracker
See which videos are gaining momentum right now in any category. Filter by format (Shorts, mid-length, long-form) to find the content style driving the most views.

📺 Channel Analyzer
Research any YouTube channel: subscriber count, average views, engagement rate, estimated monthly income, and upload frequency. Useful for competitor research or finding collaboration opportunities.

🔑 Keyword Explorer
Find keyword and title ideas ranked by search potential. Based on real YouTube video data, not generic SEO databases.

✍️ Title Generator
Generate YouTube title variations optimized for CTR based on successful titles in your niche. Copy any title with one click.

---

**WHO IS IT FOR**

- YouTube creators researching new content ideas
- Digital marketers evaluating YouTube as a channel
- Agencies running YouTube strategy for clients
- Entrepreneurs validating a niche before launching a channel

---

**HOW IT WORKS**

TubeScout uses the YouTube Data API to analyze publicly available video and channel information. It displays the results directly in a side panel while you browse YouTube — no tab switching required.

All searches are processed through our secure server. Your search history is saved locally on your device only.

---

**FREE vs PRO**

Free plan: 10 analyses per day, niche and keyword tools.
Pro plan ($4.99/month): unlimited analyses, all tools, full history, favorites, and CSV export.

---

**PRIVACY**

TubeScout only accesses YouTube pages. It does not read your Gmail, browsing history, or any personal data. It does not interact with your YouTube account. Full privacy policy: https://tube-scout-production.up.railway.app/privacy
